The Mystery of the Ascendant: Follows the strict doctrine that the sun is the ultimate enemy
. Initiates push their bodies to the limit to overcome their banes of fire and sunlight, seeking to achieve a perfected "near life" state where they can walk in the day unharmed
.
The Mystery of the Wyrm: Rejects the idea that vampirism is a curse and instead views the Requiem as a blessing
. This path focuses on taming, refining, and directing the savage Beast, making its raw power fully obedient to the Dragon's desires
.
The Mystery of the Voivode: Built on the creed that "in blood there is power," this path focuses heavily on mastering the blood bond and servitude
. Initiates aim to rise above the petty politics and slaveries of the Damned to become beings of pure, transcendent will
.
The Mystery of the Vigilant: Focuses on conquering the vulnerabilities and dangers of torpor (the sleep of ages)
. Dragons who study this path seek to wrest control of their corpses from death itself, either to attain true immortality or simply outlast their rivals
